About Streamloan.io

Streamloan.io is an early stage startup with the mission to make the “the journey to home ownership as rewarding as the destination,” by reducing friction in the residential home buying and lending processes. The web and mobile platform offers an end-to-end digital mortgage solution taking the borrower from the point of intent, purchase, to closing.

Streamloan.io was looking for a new feature to help loan officers manage loan conditions.

What's the deal with conditional loans, anyway?

Before a mortgage loan can be approved, it has to go through an underwriting process. The underwriter evaluates the loan application for worthiness and may find something in the application that requires more information before a decision can be made. These conditions can include just about anything that a lender needs to be confident that a borrower can pay the mortgage as agreed.

The Problem

How can we design a way for loan officers to manage conditions before a loan falls through?

Once a condition is sent to a borrower, the countdown begins. Loan officers have to race to get the right documents from the borrower while acting as a legalese translator and coordinator through a confusing and emotional process. If conditions aren't met in time, the borrower loses out on the loan and the loan officer loses the commision.

I believed the condition completion rate shown by the number of loan officers who are able to quickly track and assign a loan condition, would be a strong indicator of success.

The Solution

A centralized location to quickly track and manage conditions

Easily track, assign, and review loan conditions in one central place. From messaging to reminders, quickly complete the loan process without the confusion.

Final Screens
Ubelievably easy loan condition management

Quickly assign conditions, review documents, and send messages so you can spend more time closing deals.

Never miss a condition deadline

Upcoming deadline? See loan condition due dates and status at a glance.

Keep the condition process on track with built-in messages and reminders

Sending a reminders and messages to borrower is a breeze. Say goodbye to miscommunication and late documents with personalized messages and reminders.

User & Industry Research

First find business, next up translate

Loan officers = the key coordinator between the borrower & underwriter

Seven user interviews were conducted with loan officers to understand what their pain points were and which part of the loan condition process was causing the most friction.

Some the key insights were:

  • The biggest obstacle loan officers face aside from miscommunication is collecting documents from the borrower
  • Loan officers act as translators from mortgage jargon to the regular customer talk
  • Loan conditions are time-sensitive and it's crucial to view new loans sent from the loan origination system (LOS)
  • With so many players making updates to the mortgage file, the borrower doesn't always know who to send documents to.
