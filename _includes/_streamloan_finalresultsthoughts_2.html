Final Thoughts

Design for the worst case scenario

I found that it's important to break designs as soon as possible to to account for unexpected/worst-case scenerios. Loan condition copy, condition category, and borrower name (just to name a few) could all very in size and length. Starting with the worst case scenerio allowed for defined rules and better design decisions which led to a final product with a better overall experience.
