Usability Testing & Competitive Research

Users want to quickly connect with relevant issues but feel overwhelmed by the noise

In order to uncover Countable's pain points, I tested the app on 5 users at Red Rock Coffee who value following politics. I took a deep dive into competitive and pyschographic research to understand how users discover issues in political apps and how attitudes and interests inform their behavior.

Some of the key insights were:

  1. Users found that discovering relevant issues was challenging in the Countable app.
  2. 5/5 users relied on the search function to discover lawmaker profiles and current issues.
