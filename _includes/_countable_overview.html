Overview:

Take action on stories you care about

Countable makes it easier to understand the laws Congress is considering by providing bill summaries in plain English. It also streamlines the process of contacting lawmakers so voters can let their representatives know how to vote on issues that matter to them.

My interest in civic tech led me to uncover and fix Countable's usability issues within a two week sprint.
