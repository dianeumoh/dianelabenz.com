The Challenge

Design an MVP that encourages developers to certify their skills and apply for jobs

Before a software developer can apply and get hired for a role, they would need to pass a technical skill assessment on a third-party website. Knowing that the third-party redirect was unavoidable, it was clear that the flow to the technical assessment needed to be painless for the user.

I determined that application completion rate represented by the number of applicants who successfully completed the application and assessment vs. the number of the applicants who entered the stage would indicate success for the business.
