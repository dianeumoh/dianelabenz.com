User & Industry Research

Loan officers = the key coordinator between the borrower & underwriter

In 5 user interviews, loan officers consistently revealed 3 pain points:

Document Collection
Is Difficult

The biggest obstacle loan officers face is collecting documents from borrowers

Messaging Is Crucial

Loan conditions can be confusing and officers act as translators to explain mortgage loan jargon to borrowers

Reminders Are Necessary

Loan conditions are time-sensitive and it's crucial to view new conditions as soon they arrive from the loan origination system (LOS)
