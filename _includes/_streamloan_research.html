User & Industry Research

Loan officers = the key coordinator between the borrower & underwriter

Seven user interviews were conducted with loan officers to understand what their pain points were and which part of the current loan condition process was causing the most friction.

Some the key insights were:

  • The biggest obstacle loan officers face aside from miscommunication is collecting documents from the borrower
  • Loan officers act as translators from mortgage jargon to the regular customer talk
  • Loan conditions are time-sensitive and it's crucial to view new loans sent from the loan origination system (LOS)
  • With so many players making updates to the mortgage file, the borrower doesn't always know who to send documents to.
