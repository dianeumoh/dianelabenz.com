Design Iterations

Developing The Conditions Flow

According to user interviews and industry research, loan officers need help accomplishing several key tasks:

  1. See New Conditions from the loan origination system (LOS)
  2. Assign Loan Conditions to the borrower
  3. Accept Or Decline Documents uploaded by the borrower
  4. Email Accepted Conditions to the LOS for approval from the underwriter
Iteration 1: Defining submenus through information architecture

Early iterations translated key tasks that a loan officer needs to complete into submenu categories.

Iteration 2: Consolidating submenus & adding visual cues

The Assigned submenu was removed after realizing the user could view assigned conditions in the Review tab. Clock icons and due dates were added to the detail views to help a loan officer track conditions at a glance.

Iteration 3: From "Approved" to "Sent"

Validation testing with loan officers revealed that they are not legally allowed to approve loan conditions (only underwriters can). The Sent submenu was created to allow loan officers to see which loans were sent to the loan origination system (LOS).
