Industry & Competitive Research

Software developers screen jobs by technology tags

I analyzed competitors and analogous platforms in the US and Asian markets to understand how developers searched and applied for jobs and how technical knowledge is verified.

I grouped my research findings into a few different categories:

Job Search

Software developers screened for jobs using technology tabs to see if the position was interesting and if they fit the description.

Application & Profile

Applications only asked for the most necessary information.

Users are allowed to complete the process at their own pace.

Home Dashboard

Home page dashboards employed visual nudges to guide the user throughout each step in the job search process.
