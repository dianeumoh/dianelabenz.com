Validation Test Learnings

"Where do I upload my resume?"

The final prototype was tested on 5 junior developers in the Bay Area. I found that 5/5 developers expected to upload their resume and cover letter before submitting their application.

Since the lack of context could discourage users from applying, I knew it was important to update.

Final Thoughts

MVP: A balancing act of the present and future

Deciding on minimum viable features needed for an MVP recruitment platform required a careful balance of present and future planning. I worked closely with the founder to better understand how design decisions would scale and accounted for immediate business needs and technical level of effort.

Design systems are living entities

I learned that a design system doesn’t always make sense to have for every project. Instead of trying to anticipate design decisions and incorporate it into a design system, I found that it’s best to evolve the design system as needs arise.
