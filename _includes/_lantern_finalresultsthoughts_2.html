Deciding On Minimum Viable Features

It was a challenge to prioritize the minimum viable features needed for a recruitment platform MVP.

Building out a design system

I learned that a design system doesn’t always make sense to have for every project. Instead of trying to anticipate design decisions and incorporate it into a design system, I found that it’s best to address any issues as they come up.
